About Me

“What lies behind us and what lies before us are small matters compared to what lies within us. And when we bring what is within us out into the world, miracles happen.” Henry Stanley Haskins

In some ways I feel I have lived a few lifetimes in terms of my path and where I am now. Much of my life was spent growing up and living in California where I managed an interior design firm in San Diego and ultimately owned and operated my own window treatment/interior design company. After moving to Indiana, I changed directions which equated to sales and distribution of wine and spirits. Eventually one of my real passions was called upon which was helping people in recovery through coaching and a therapeutic modality called Internal Family Systems. In the background of this journey, I was always dabbling in graphic design in a multitude of ways.

At this point I am attempting to honor the marriage of my interests. I am committed to furthering JoyFoli Designed® and my web development career with 1150 Academy, along with growing my Coaching and Internal Family Systems practice, JoyFoli Integrated®. Interestingly they both have some commonality regarding utilizing systems to work together and create more balance or desired outcomes. For me the joy that comes from creating things and supporting people in their journey is accessed through both ventures.
